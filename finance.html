My Finance Forecasts

How to Play

  • Select Today or Tomorrow from the forecast day selector.
  • Enter your forecast for the asset price before the cutoff time.
  • Feel free to use market data, web search, LLMs, or your own analysis to help guide your forecasts.
  • Click Save to save your forecasts. You can edit forecasts until the cutoff times for each asset.
  • Click View My Score the next morning to see verified results and your shareable score. Good luck!
ⓘ Dates shown in Eastern Time (ET)
